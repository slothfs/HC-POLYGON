Choso from Jujutsu Kaisen

Choso from Jujutsu Kaisen is one of those characters who feels cold and dangerous at first, but the more you see him, the more emotional and real he becomes. He fights using Blood Manipulation, which makes his battles look insanely cool, but what really makes people like him is how much he cares about his brothers and how loyal he is to Yuji Itadori later in the story. Choso has a sad past, a calm personality, and some of the best moments in the series, which is why so many fans think he's one of the best-written characters in Jujutsu Kaisen
